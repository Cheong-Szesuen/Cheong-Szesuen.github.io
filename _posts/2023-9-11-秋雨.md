<center>没想到</center>
<center>天居然</center>
<center>会愈来愈冷</center>
<br>
<center>就像曾经</center>
<center>在太阳下</center>
<center>贪婪着温暖</center>
<center>从未想过</center>
<center>明天会怎样</center>
<br>
<center>如果明天</center>
<center>就是寒冰霜冻</center>
<center>那就让我</center>
<center>离开这没有温柔的天地</center>
<center>即日</center>
<center>飞往南国</center>
<br>
<center>不是我忍受不了</center>
<center>这般的严寒</center>
<center>而是我无法忍受</center>
<center>这样的冷漠</center>
<br>
